**Data:**  
Extensive energy monitoring data combined with IT workload metrics from operational data center facilities, requiring integration of time-series energy consumption with computational load data.

**Approach:**

- Implemented feature engineering to extract temporal patterns and load-efficiency relationships
- Deployed LightGBM for regression (PUE prediction) and Gradient Boosting for multi-class efficiency classification

**Outcome/Impact:**  
- Achieved **0.882 R² score** with LightGBM regression model for PUE prediction

- Identified critical operational factors affecting energy efficiency, providing actionable insights for real-time monitoring system integration
- Demonstrated potential for **15-20% operational cost reduction** through targeted optimization of identified high-impact variables
